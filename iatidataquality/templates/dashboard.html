Publish What You Fund has developed two new tools to assess the quality of organisations' publication:

1) A Data Quality tool has been developed to assess the quality of aid information published in IATI-XML format. It is designed to run a series of automated checks and tests on data published in IATI-XML format and present findings in a way that provides both a comparative view across all organisations and granular level details on each organisation’s data and any gaps.

2) A survey tool has been developed to assess aid information published by organisations in formats other than IATI XML, based on data collected manually for the annual Aid Transparency Index.

More information on both tools can be found by clicking on the 'About’ tab above. Data from individual organisations can be accessed from the 'organisations' link below.
